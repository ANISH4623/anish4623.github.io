About Us

Dr. Marcus Rodriguez - Prosthodontist

Dr. Rodriguez is a skilled prosthodontist at 2nd Avenue Dental, specializing in the restoration and replacement of teeth. With a keen eye for detail, he excels in crafting natural-looking smiles through advanced restorative procedures.

Dr. Rodriguez's expertise extends to the latest in dental implants, crowns, and bridges. His commitment to precision and aesthetics makes him a trusted professional for those seeking comprehensive restorative dental solutions.
Dr. Emily Chang - Pediatric Dentist

Dr. Chang is the caring pediatric dentist at 2nd Avenue Dental, specializing in children's dental health. Her gentle approach and friendly demeanor create a positive and comfortable experience for young patients.

Dr. Chang's commitment to continuing education in pediatric dentistry ensures that she stays informed about the latest advancements in child-friendly treatments, making her an essential part of the practice's commitment to family dentistry.
Dr. Alexander Turner - Head Dentist and Founder

Dr. Turner is the visionary leader and founder of 2nd Avenue Dental. With a rich history spanning over two decades in dentistry, he has devoted his career to delivering exceptional dental care with a strong emphasis on personalized treatment plans.

Beyond his technical prowess, Dr. Turner is recognized for his warm and approachable demeanor, instilling a sense of comfort in his patients. His overarching philosophy centers on comprehensive dentistry, not only addressing oral health but also focusing on the holistic well-being of his patients.
